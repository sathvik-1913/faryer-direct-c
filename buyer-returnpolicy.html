Return & Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of our farm-fresh products. Learn about our hassle-free return process.

Policy Overview

At Farmer Direct, we take pride in delivering the freshest farm products directly to your doorstep. We understand that sometimes products may not meet your expectations, and we're committed to making things right. Our return policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and customer-friendly.

Return Timeframe

You can return eligible products within the following timeframes:

  • Fresh Produce: 24 hours from delivery
  • Packaged Goods: 7 days from delivery
  • Damaged Items: 48 hours from delivery
Note: For fresh produce, we recommend inspecting your items immediately upon delivery and reporting any issues within the first few hours for the best resolution.

What Can Be Returned

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • Damaged or broken items during delivery
  • Wrong items delivered
  • Significantly poor quality (rotten, spoiled)
  • Expired products
  • Missing items from your order

What Cannot Be Returned

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns for:

  • Products that have been partially consumed
  • Items that have been stored improperly after delivery
  • Products that show normal signs of ripening or aging
  • Items purchased during clearance sales
  • Personal preference changes (you simply didn't like the taste)
Important: Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns for items that have been refrigerated or frozen after delivery, unless they were delivered in that condition.

Return Process

1

Report Issue

Contact us within the specified timeframe with your order details and issue description

2

Get Approval

Our team will review your request and approve eligible returns within 4 hours

3

Schedule Pickup

We'll arrange a convenient time to collect the return items from your location

4

Receive Refund

Get your refund processed to original payment method within 3-5 business days

Refund Information

Once your return is approved and processed, you can expect:

  • Full Refund: For damaged, wrong, or missing items
  • Partial Refund: For quality issues where partial product is usable
  • Store Credit: Option to receive credit for future purchases
  • Replacement: For eligible items, we can send a replacement instead of refund

Refund Timeline: Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned items. The time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your payment method and bank processing times.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I receive a damaged product?
Take photos of the damaged product and packaging immediately. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and photos. We'll arrange a pickup and process a full refund or replacement.
Do I need to keep the original packaging?
For damaged or wrong items, please keep the original packaging as it helps us investigate the issue with our delivery partners. For quality issues, packaging is not required.
Can I return products I don't like?
Due to the nature of fresh produce, we cannot accept returns based on personal taste preferences. However, we welcome your feedback to help us improve our product selection and quality.
What about returns for subscription orders?
Subscription orders follow the same return policy. You can return any delivery from your subscription following the standard process. You can also pause or cancel your subscription at any time.
Who pays for return shipping?
We cover all return shipping costs for eligible returns. Our delivery partner will pick up the items from your location at no charge to you.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer support team is here to help you with any return-related questions or issues. Contact us and we'll guide you through the process.

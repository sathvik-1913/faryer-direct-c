Shipping Policy

Fresh from our farm to your doorstep

Our Shipping Commitment

At Farmer's Market, we understand that freshness is paramount when it comes to farm-fresh produce. That's why we've developed a specialized shipping process to ensure your items arrive in perfect condition.

We harvest most items the same day they ship, guaranteeing maximum freshness and nutritional value.

Shipping Options & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping

$5.99

3-5 business days

Perfect for non-perishable items and pantry staples.

Express Shipping

$12.99

1-2 business days

Ideal for most fresh produce and dairy products.

Overnight Shipping

$19.99

Next business day

Recommended for highly perishable items like berries and leafy greens.

Note: We do not ship on weekends to ensure product quality. Orders placed after 2 PM local time will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Areas

We currently ship to all 50 states in the U.S. Shipping times may vary based on your location:

  • Local (within 100 miles): 1-2 business days with standard shipping
  • Regional (100-500 miles): 2-3 business days with standard shipping
  • National (500+ miles): 3-5 business days with standard shipping

We're working to expand our shipping capabilities to international locations. Please check back for updates.

Packaging & Freshness Guarantee

All our shipments include specialized packaging to maintain freshness:

  • Insulated boxes with temperature control
  • Biodegradable cooling packs for perishables
  • Breathable packaging for produce that needs air circulation
  • Secure packaging to prevent damage during transit

If your items arrive damaged or not fresh, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this to monitor your package's journey from our farm to your home.

For orders with multiple items that ship separately, you'll receive tracking information for each shipment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free shipping? +

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, standard shipping is $5.99.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering? +

You can change your shipping address within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting our customer service team. After that time, we cannot guarantee address changes as orders enter our fulfillment process.

What if I'm not home when my package arrives? +

Our packages are designed to maintain temperature for several hours. However, we recommend having someone available to bring perishable items indoors promptly. For security, you can also provide delivery instructions during checkout.

Do you ship to PO boxes? +

We can only ship non-perishable items to PO boxes. Perishable items require a physical address for delivery.

What is your policy during extreme weather? +

During extreme weather conditions that could compromise product quality, we may delay shipments. In such cases, we will notify you promptly and work to ship your order as soon as conditions improve.
